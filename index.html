About Woodland Building & Renovations LLC

Woodland Building & Renovations LLC was started in 2010 by Ted Wrubel with a primary focus on executing complex and detailed alterations and custom designs within a clients home with minimal impact. With us, you can expect high quality professionalism through every step of the renovation process: precise scheduling of subcontractors, timely completion of projects, and a guarantee that your home will be respected and protected. Getting the job right the first time is something that we at Woodland Building & Renovations take very seriously. In addition to the Connecticut State Building Code, Woodland Building & Renovations LLC constructs new homes that exceed the requirements of the Energy Conservation Code. When constructed in this fashion, these homes will save the homeowner thousands of dollars by employing the leading technology in insulation, air sealing, and high quality windows providing a very comfortable dream home and reducing your carbon footprint for years to come.
